platform notes
iOS ↔ Android deltas
Corners & chrome
iOS ~42px superellipse; Android ~30px + gesture pill. Status bar right-aligned battery % on Android.
Navigation bar
iOS floating tab bar with elevated accent circle. Android Material 3 nav: pill indicator behind the active icon, taller bar, ripple on tap.
Top bar
Android uses a contained app bar with leading menu icon; iOS keeps logo + inline controls.
Shared
Palette, type, glass surfaces, spore-green CTAs, teal identify, amber tokens — identical across both.
