SkyForest is a weather assistant for mushroom foragers. It analyzes weather conditions at your favorite spots and tells you when the odds of a good harvest are highest.

How it works:
- Save your foraging locations on the map.
- Record your "best days" — successful trips with the species you found.
- SkyForest compares current and forecast weather against your best days and gives you a match score, so you know when to head out.

Features:
- 14-day weather analysis and rain map for any location.
- Pattern comparison against your golden days.
- Automatic monitoring with alerts when conditions line up.
- Forest search by radius, land cover and tree species.
- Mushroom species reference powered by iNaturalist.
- Community marketplace for anonymized weather patterns.
- Guided mushroom tours with a token-based auction.

SkyForest works worldwide and helps beginners and seasoned pickers make smarter decisions about when and where to forage.

Some features use in-app tokens, which can be purchased inside the app.
